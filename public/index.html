Goal: Be the first to get rid of all your cards!

Setup: Each player gets 3 face-down cards, 3 face-up cards on top, and 3-5 cards in hand.

Special Cards:

  • 2: Wild card - resets the rank
  • 7: Next player must play lower
  • 8: Skips next player
  • 10: Burns the pile, play again
  • 4 of a kind: Auto-burns the pile

Rules: Play equal or higher than the pile. If you can't play, pick up the pile.
